After years of my personal website being a redirect to GitHub, I finally built something real.

## Why now?

I wanted a place to:

1. **Learn in public** — TIL notes inspired by [Simon Willison](https://til.simonwillison.net/)
2. **Write longer pieces** — Not everything fits in a tweet
3. **Own my content** — Not locked into any platform

## The stack

- **Astro** — Static site generator, fast and simple
- **Markdown** — For all content
- **Cloudflare Pages** — Free hosting with great DX
- **Buttondown** — Newsletter (coming soon)

## What's next

- More TIL posts as I learn
- Longer blog posts when I have something to say
- Maybe some project write-ups
